Add a cover image to your song and upload it to YouTube.

Free, no watermark

Easy convert and upload songs to YouTube

Upload mp3 to YouTube, add an image to a song

Work is in progress…

In a while, here will be a web application that will allow you to upload a song, an album, a mix, or a playlist to YouTube in an effortless way:

  1. Pick a cover image (png or jpg) on your device
  2. Pick a song on your device
  3. Preview and edit the description that will be added to the YouTube video
  4. Click the “Upload” button
  5. Watch an ad, meanwhile
  6. YtUploader will convert your image and song to a video file and will upload it to your YouTube channel. The video won't have any watermarks

Add the cover image to the whole album and upload a playlist or a single album video

If you want to upload a whole album or playlist, the flow is similar:

  1. Pick a cover image (png or jpg) on your device
  2. Pick songs from your device
  3. YtUploader will automatically generate the description for the album video with timecodes.
  4. Preview and edit the description that will be added to the YouTube video
  5. Choose if you want to upload the whole album as a single video, or each song separately, and create a playlist for them. Or both
  6. Click the “Upload” button
  7. Watch an ad, meanwhile
  8. The YtUploader will convert your image and song to one or several video files and upload them to your YouTube channel. The videos won't have any watermarks
